Trivit is a simple tally counter app for counting anything - inventory, habits, scores, or any other counting needs.

No login required. All data is stored locally on device.

To test:
1. Launch the app to see the counter list
2. Tap + to create a new counter
3. Tap a counter to increment/decrement
4. Swipe to delete counters
5. Use context menus for additional options

The app works fully offline and requires no special credentials.
