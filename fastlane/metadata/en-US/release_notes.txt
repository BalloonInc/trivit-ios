Trivit 5.0 - A Complete Reimagining

This is a ground-up rewrite of Trivit using SwiftUI, Apple's modern framework for building beautiful apps.

What's New:

- Apple Watch App: Track your tallies right from your wrist with the all-new watchOS app

- Real-Time Sync: Your counts sync instantly between iPhone and Apple Watch

- 8 Color Themes: Beautiful new color palette to personalize your tallies

- Faster Performance: Optimized for the latest iOS with smoother animations

- Modern Design: Refined interface that's cleaner and more intuitive

- Open Source: View the code and contribute at https://github.com/BalloonInc/trivit-ios

Thank you for using Trivit!
