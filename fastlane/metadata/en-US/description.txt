Trivit is a beautifully simple tally counter app that helps you track anything in your life. Whether you're counting habits, workouts, daily goals, or inventory, Trivit makes it effortless and enjoyable.

SIMPLE AND INTUITIVE
Just tap to count. No complicated setup, no learning curve. Create a new tally in seconds and start tracking immediately. The clean, distraction-free interface keeps you focused on what matters.

APPLE WATCH APP
Track your tallies right from your wrist with the all-new Apple Watch app. Your counts sync instantly between iPhone and Apple Watch, so you're always up to date no matter which device you use.

BEAUTIFUL COLOR THEMES
Choose from 8 vibrant color themes to personalize your tallies. Each counter gets its own distinctive color, making it easy to identify at a glance. The colorful, flat design is both beautiful and functional.

TRACK ANYTHING
- Daily habits and routines
- Workout reps and sets
- Inventory counts
- Score keeping for games
- Bird watching or wildlife sightings
- Glasses of water consumed
- Steps toward a goal
- Anything else you need to count!

SYNC ACROSS ALL DEVICES
Your tallies automatically sync across all your devices using iCloud. Start counting on your iPhone and continue on your Apple Watch, or vice versa. Your data is always with you.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays on your devices and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect any personal information, and there are no analytics or tracking. Your counts are your business.

NO ADS, NO SUBSCRIPTIONS
Trivit is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. Just a great app that does what it says.

OPEN SOURCE
Trivit is open source! You can view the code, suggest improvements, or contribute on GitHub at https://github.com/BalloonInc/trivit-ios
