# Trivit - New Feature Ideas

This document contains brainstormed features for future development of Trivit. These are ideas to be reviewed and prioritized together.

---

## Priority 1: Core Experience Enhancements

### 1. Quick Increment Widget Actions
- **Description**: Add interactive widget buttons to increment directly from home screen
- **Platform**: iOS 17+
- **Benefit**: Fastest possible counting without opening the app
- **Effort**: Medium

### 2. Apple Watch Complications
- **Description**: Show count on watch face, tap to open trivit
- **Complication Types**: Circular, Rectangular, Corner, Inline
- **Benefit**: Glanceable access to most-used counters
- **Effort**: Medium

### 3. Siri Shortcuts Integration
- **Description**: Voice commands to increment/read counts
- **Examples**: "Hey Siri, add one to coffee", "How many push-ups today?"
- **Benefit**: Hands-free counting
- **Effort**: Medium

### 4. Focus Mode Filtering
- **Description**: Show different trivits based on active Focus mode
- **Examples**: Work trivits during work hours, fitness during exercise
- **Benefit**: Contextual relevance
- **Effort**: Low

## Liquid Glass Investigation

### Current Status
Apple's Liquid Glass design language was introduced in iOS 18. We should evaluate:

1. **Adoption level**: How widely is it used in Apple's apps?
2. **User reception**: Is it well-received?
3. **Our brand**: Does it fit Trivit's colorful, playful aesthetic?
4. **Implementation**: SwiftUI support level?

### Recommendation
Keep our current custom UI as it's distinctive and works well. Consider Liquid Glass for:
- Modal sheets
- Settings panels
- System-style overlays

But retain our bold, colorful row design as our signature look.
